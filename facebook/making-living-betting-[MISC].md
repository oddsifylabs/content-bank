# Can you make a living from sports betting? [MISC]

**Yes, but it's harder than you think.**

**The math:**

To make $50k/year at 5% ROI (excellent):
- Need $1M annual volume
- At 1% bet sizing = $100k bankroll
- Or: $50k bankroll + 10% ROI (very rare)

**The reality:**

Full-time bettors:
- Treat it like a business (8hrs/day research)
- Have 6+ figure bankrolls
- Use 5+ sportsbooks + multiple accounts
- Track every bet with CLV
- Never deviate from process

**Most people:**
- Start with $500-2k bankroll
- Bet 5-10% per game (too much)
- Use 1-2 books (no line shopping)
- Chase losses (emotional)
- Quit within 6 months

---

**Better path:**

1. Keep your day job
2. Build bankroll to $20k+ (side income)
3. Prove profitability over 500+ bets
4. *Then* consider going full-time
