# Why do books offer parlay insurance? [EV]

**Because parlays are their biggest profit center.**

**House edge:**
- Singles: 4.5%
- 2-leg parlay: 12%
- 3-leg parlay: 15-20%

**Parlay insurance** = "Lose one leg, get your stake back."

Sounds generous. Here's the catch:

**You still lose time + opportunity cost:**
- Spent 30 min researching 3 legs
- One loses (the 40% that always lose)
- Get $10 back, lost the *profit* you could've made elsewhere

**Books know:**
- Parlay bettors chase big scores
- Insurance keeps them coming back
- Long-term: parlay bettors lose more than single bettors

---

**Sharp alternative:**
- Bet 3 singles instead of 1 parlay
- 2-1 record = profit even with loss
- CLV trackable (parlays aren't)

---

**Promotions are for entertainment, not profit.**
