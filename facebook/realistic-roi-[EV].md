# What's a realistic ROI for sports betting? [EV]

**Sharp bettors: 3-7% ROI long-term.**

**Breakdown:**

| Win Rate | ROI at -110 | Verdict |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| 50% | -4.5% | Losing (vig kills you) |
| 52.4% | 0% | Break even |
| 54% | +3% | Good |
| 55% | +5% | Excellent |
| 57%+ | +9%+ | Elite (unsustainable?) |

---

**Reality check:**

55% win rate over 500 bets = top 1% of bettors.

Most "60%+ this month!" accounts:
- Small sample size (20 bets)
- Delete losses (no audit trail)
- Parlay luck (not repeatable)

---

**Our standard:** EV ≥4% per bet.  
Some hit, some miss. Long-term: 5-6% ROI.
