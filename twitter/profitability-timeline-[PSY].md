# How long until I'm profitable at sports betting? [PSY]

**Honest answer: 18-24 months minimum.**

Here's the timeline:

**Months 1-6:** Learning variance
- You'll have 3-5 losing streaks
- Question if the process works
- Most people quit here

**Months 7-12:** Process refinement
- Rules become automatic
- CLV tracking feels natural
- Win rate stabilizes

**Months 13-18:** First real profits
- Variance evens out
- 55%+ win rate if process is sound
- Bankroll growth visible

**Months 19-24:** Compounding
- Profits accelerate
- Confidence (not arrogance) builds
- You've survived multiple drawdowns

---

**The filter:**

~5-8% of people who start make it to Month 18.

Not because it's hard. Because people:
- Increase bet size during losses
- Abandon process during downswings
- Chase "sure things" outside the system
