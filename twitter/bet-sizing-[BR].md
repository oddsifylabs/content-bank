# How much should I bet per game? [BR]

**The 1% Rule:**

Bankroll: $1,000 → Max bet: $10 (1 unit)  
Bankroll: $5,000 → Max bet: $50 (1 unit)  
Bankroll: $10,000 → Max bet: $100 (1 unit)

**Why?**

Even at 55% win rate (elite), you'll have 10-20% drawdowns.

At 1%: 20% drawdown = -20 units. Painful, recoverable.  
At 5%: 20% drawdown = -100 units. Game over.
