# What does CLV mean? [CLV]

**Short answer:** Closing Line Value = whether you beat the market.

**Long answer:**

You bet Chiefs -3 at 10am.  
Game starts at 4pm with Chiefs -4.5.  
**You got 1.5 points better than the closing line.**

That's positive CLV. You won the trade, even if the bet loses.

---

**Why it matters:**

Over 500+ bets, positive CLV = profitable.  
Negative CLV = losing long-term.

Win/loss record on individual bets? Noise.  
CLV hit rate? Signal.
