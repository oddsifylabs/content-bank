# Why do sharp bettors care about CLV? [CLV]

**Because CLV predicts long-term profit better than win/loss record.**

Think of it like stock trading:
- You buy at $50, it's worth $52 at close → you made a good trade
- Even if you sell at $48 later (bad timing), the *initial trade* was +EV

Same with betting:
- You bet -3, line closes -4.5 → you beat the market
- Even if team loses by 4 (bad luck), you made a good bet

---

**The data:**

60%+ CLV hit rate over 100+ bets = profitable long-term.  
<50% CLV hit rate = losing long-term (even with 55% win rate).

---
